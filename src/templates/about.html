Motivation.

The other day I was waiting for someone in a street and in the meanwhile I thought -"I don't know too much about the person who is giving the name to this street. It would be great to geolocalize my position, using, for instance, my smartphone and easily get information about the person from the wikipedia."

Code.

The app is coded using Openlayers, brython and flask.

The repo is located here in case you want to report an issue, request a new feature, fork it or say hello.
